Overview

Population (2025)

68 million people across four nations, making it Europe's third-most populous country with rich cultural diversity and heritage.

Location & Geography

Island nation in Northwestern Europe, comprising England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean, North Sea, English Channel, and Irish Sea.

Capital & Major Cities

London (capital). Major cities: Edinburgh, Cardiff, Belfast, Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, Liverpool.

Government

Constitutional monarchy with parliamentary democracy. Head of State: King Charles III. Head of Government: Prime Minister.

Global Influence & Legacy

The United Kingdom has shaped global history through the British Empire, now transformed into the Commonwealth of Nations. It remains a permanent member of the UN Security Council, nuclear power, and leading financial center with enduring cultural impact worldwide.

Royal Heritage

The British Monarchy

The UK is a constitutional monarchy with over 1,000 years of royal history. The current monarch, King Charles III, ascended to the throne in 2022 following the historic 70-year reign of Queen Elizabeth II.

The Royal Family represents national unity and tradition, while the UK Parliament holds legislative power. This unique balance has inspired constitutional systems worldwide.

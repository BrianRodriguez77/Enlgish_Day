🇨🇭 Overview

Population (2025)

8.9 million inhabitants living in one of the world's most prosperous and peaceful nations, known for exceptional quality of life.

Location

Central Europe, landlocked between France, Germany, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein. 60% covered by the majestic Alps.

Capital & Cities

Bern (capital). Major cities: Zürich, Geneva, Basel, Lausanne, Lucerne.

Languages

Four official languages: German (62%), French (23%), Italian (8%), Romansh (0.5%). Multilingualism is a cornerstone of Swiss identity.

Direct Democracy Pioneer

Switzerland practices one of the world's most advanced forms of direct democracy, where citizens vote on national issues 4 times per year. Neutrality since 1815 has made it a global hub for diplomacy and peace.

🏔️ 26 Cantons

Federal Structure

Switzerland is a confederation of 26 cantons, each with its own constitution, parliament, and government. This unique federal system ensures strong regional autonomy while maintaining national unity.

🏔️ Alpine Majesty

The Swiss Alps

Home to 48 peaks over 4,000 meters, including the iconic Matterhorn (4,478m) and Jungfrau. The Alps cover 60% of Switzerland's territory.

World-class ski resorts like Zermatt, St. Moritz, and Verbier attract millions of visitors annually for winter sports and summer hiking.

💰 Economy & Innovation

Global Excellence

Switzerland boasts one of the world's highest GDP per capita ($92,000+ USD), ranking among the most competitive and innovative economies globally.

  • Banking & Finance — Zürich and Geneva are global financial centers
  • Pharmaceuticals — Novartis, Roche leading healthcare innovation
  • Luxury Watches — Rolex, Patek Philippe, Omega
  • Precision Manufacturing — World-renowned quality
  • Innovation Hub — Top 3 in Global Innovation Index

Swiss Quality

The "Swiss Made" label represents excellence worldwide. From chocolate (Lindt, Toblerone) to precision instruments, Swiss products set global standards for quality and craftsmanship.
